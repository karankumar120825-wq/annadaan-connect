🔄 The Process

How Annadaan Connect Works

A simple, fast process that turns your celebration's surplus into someone's meal.

1
📝

Register Your Event

Sign up and register your wedding, birthday, corporate event, or any celebration with details like date, venue, and expected food quantity.

2
📢

Alert Connected NGOs

Instantly notify NGOs in your area about the surplus food. Share location, food type, and pickup window directly through the platform.

3
🤝

NGO Confirms Pickup

Your nearest partner NGO confirms the collection, assigns volunteers, and coordinates with you on the right time to pick up the food.

4
😊

Food Reaches the Needy

The collected food is distributed to shelters, orphanages, and underprivileged communities. You receive a confirmation with impact stats.
